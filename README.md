## 🧬 About Me
I am a Biochemical Engineer and PhD Candidate specializing in the mechanistic modeling of complex dynamic systems. My expertise lies in formulating and solving Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs) to describe spatial and temporal dynamics. 

With a strong foundation in compartmental mass balances, numerical optimization, and parameter constraint strategies, I am transitioning my bioprocess modeling expertise toward developing robust **Pharmacokinetics/Pharmacodynamics (PK/PD)** architectures and biostatistical workflows.

## 🛠️ Core Competencies
* **Mathematical Modeling:** Compartmental modeling, Non-linear ODEs, Transition from linear to saturation/inhibition kinetics (Michaelis-Menten equivalent).
* **Scientific Computing:** Python (SciPy, NumPy, Pandas), Wolfram Mathematica.
* **Optimization & Biostatistics:** Nonlinear regression, Bounded optimization, Parameter estimation, Survival Analysis.
* **Data Engineering:** Automated data extraction, deterministic workflows, and reproducible pipelines.

### 5. [Data Wrangling & Deterministic Quality Control](./data-engineering/data-cleaning/)
* **Focus:** Data Quality / Reproducibility.
